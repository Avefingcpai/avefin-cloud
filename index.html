Solution overview

Avefin is a cloud-native platform that gives small and mid-sized businesses rapid access to working-capital solutions‚Äîincluding simple credit lines, revolving credit and invoice factoring‚Äî while providing a clear, data-driven view of their financial health.

The platform is built end-to-end on Google Cloud. Financial documents and invoices are uploaded to Cloud Storage, transactional data is stored in Cloud SQL, and portfolio-wide analytics run in BigQuery. This creates a single, secure data layer that powers every financing decision.

How Avefin uses Google Cloud
  • Vertex AI automates underwriting and risk scoring, combining cash-flow signals, payment behavior, and sector benchmarks.
  • Cloud Run hosts low-latency decision APIs that return eligibility and pricing in minutes instead of days.
  • Cloud Functions & Pub/Sub orchestrate document intake, KYC checks and payout workflows with full audit trails.
  • IAM and Cloud KMS enforce tenant isolation, strong encryption and compliance for financial data in Latin America.

Architecture & value

By standardizing all credit and factoring workflows on Google Cloud, Avefin can evaluate thousands of SME applications in parallel, scale automatically to peak demand, and maintain strong controls around sensitive financial data.

This architecture reduces manual underwriting, lowers risk, and lets SMEs obtain transparent, flexible financing up to $10M with predictable, data-backed pricing.

  • Operations: Applications move from submission to funding in less than 72 hours, with status tracking powered by BigQuery dashboards.
  • Analytics: Portfolio-level insights on concentration risk, sector exposure, and payment behavior inform smarter credit policies.
  • Security: End-to-end encryption, role-based access control and audit logging protect borrower data and support regulatory compliance.
